How to Make Spaghetti

  1. Put meatballs in oven with a 30 minute timer.
  2. Boil water.
  3. Put noodles in boiling water.
  4. Wait 10 minutes for noodles to cook.
  5. Strain noodles.
  6. Put sauce in pot and wait till hot.
  7. Put cooked noodle in sauce pot.
  8. Put meatballs in sauce pot.
  9. Serve and enjoy the spaghetti!!!.
